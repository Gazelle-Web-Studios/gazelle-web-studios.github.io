Lawn Treatments

Seasonal treatments designed to feed and protect your lawn throughout the year.
  • Why? Boosts lawn health, colour, and resilience.
  • When? We offer a treatment for each season that will keep your grass growing strong year round!
  • How? Applied by professionals using safe, effective products.

Moss & Weed Control

Targeted treatment to remove unwanted moss and weeds and promote healthier grass coverage.
  • Why? Maintains a clean, lush lawn free of invasive growth.
  • When? As soon as weeds or moss are detected. The earlier the better!
  • How? All of our operatives are fully trained and hold PA1 and PA6 qualifications. They will apply both herbicides and moss-killer to your lawn and then remove the leftover.
Say goodbye to weeds

Aeration

Aeration involves perforating the soil to allow better penetration of air, water, and nutrients.
  • Why? Improves root growth and combats soil compaction.
  • When? Ideal in Spring or Autumn when lawns are actively growing.
  • How? We use a Bannerman Aerator machine to create holes in your lawn that are perfectly placed to combat soil compaction.
Book a lawn survey today

Scarification

Scarification removes built-up thatch from your lawn to restore access to air, water, and nutrients.
  • Why? Prevents suffocation of your lawn and improves growth.
  • When? Best in Spring and Autumn.
  • How? We use an Eliet machine that expertly removes all thatch without causing any unnecessary damage to your lawn. We will perform two passes with the machine to ensure that all of the unwanted plant matter is removed.
Interested in a free lawn survey?

Lawn Renovation

Comprehensive recovery for lawns suffering from damage, thinning, or neglect.
  • Why? Revives tired lawns and restores vibrant coverage.
  • When? Best done in Spring or early Autumn, after sun damage from Summer or frost damage in the Winter.
  • How? First, we apply our moss and weed killer treatment. 7-14 days later, we will return to aerate, seed, and top dress your lawn. Then, we will apply an effective fertiliser. This is a much more cost effective way to restore your lawn than re-turfing.
Let us restore your lawn

Overseeding

Introducing fresh grass seed to existing turf to improve density and colour.
  • Why? Thickens thin areas and improves overall lawn health.
  • When? Best results in Spring or Autumn.
  • How? Seeds are scattered across your lawn using a spreading machine. This ensures even placement of seeding across your lawn to remove any patchy areas.
Book overseeding now

Top Dressing

A layer of sand or soil applied to improve lawn level and soil structure.
  • Why? Helps smooth out uneven surfaces and improves lawn level, compost structure and soil health.
  • When? Usually performed after aeration or scarification.
  • How? Applied evenly across your lawn by hand or machine.

Disease Control

Expert identification and treatment of lawn diseases like red thread, fusarium, and more.
  • Why? Stops the spread of disease and protects lawn health.
  • When? As soon as signs of disease appear — early treatment is key.
  • How? Our specialists diagnose and treat with precision and care.

Pest Control

Expert identification and treatment of lawn pests like leatherjackets and chafers.
  • Why? Northamptonshire has clay soil, which is iideal for pests such as leatherjackets and chafers. These pests will wreak havoc on your lawn.
  • When? As soon as signs of infestation appear — early treatment is key.
  • How? Our specialists will apply a pest control solution, unique to the pest that has infested your lawn.
